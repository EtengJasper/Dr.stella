Profile

I'm Stella Eteng-Uket a Pychometrician and a lecturer in the Department of Educational Psychology, Guidance & Counseling, University of Port Harcourt, Nigeria. I obtained my Bachelor’s, Masters and Ph.D. degrees from the University of Port Harcourt. As an expert and authority in my field, I teach courses in Test and Measurement, Test Development & Administration, Psychological Testing, Psychology of Learning and others. Am a consultant and facilitator for educational and financial institutions. I have numerous scholarly publications published in reputable journals both within and out sides the shores of Nigeria. In the same vein I have attended various workshops and seminars in my field. Am also a member of professional bodies like the Organization for Women in Science for the Developing World (OWSD), International Association for Innovations in Educational Assessment (IAIEA) and Association of Educational Researchers and Evaluators of Nigeria (ASSEREN).

RESEARCH COMPLETED BUT NOT PUBLISHED

1. Revalidation of Test of General Reasoning.

Objectives: The research objective was to revalidate the Test of General Reasoning( TOGRA) for use in Nigeria
Findings: The research was able to revalidate the Test of General Reasoning for use in Nigeria. Specifically, it was able to determine the properties of Reliability( test retest, alternate), Validity ( Construct, Hypothesis Evidence) and Item analysis ( Difficulty index, Discriminatory index, DIF) of TOGRA for use in Nigeria using Classical Test Theory and Item Response Theory

2. Evaluation of Post Graduate Diploma Programme in Education.

Objectives: The research objective was to evaluate the Objectives of the Post Graduate Diploma Programe in Education (PGDE), in the University of Port Harcourt.
Findings: The evaluation result revealed that the objectives of the Post Graduate Diploma Programe in Education is being achieved and it was recommended that the PGDE programme should continue with modification especially on the facilities available for implementation for more effective objective realization
